Game Mechanics

Evasive

The creature's attacks cannot be blocked, unless the defender is also Evasive.

Excessive

If the attacker's attack is greater than the HP of the defender, the difference is dealt to the defender's owner's HP.

Deft

If the attacker's attack is greater than the HP of the defender, the attacker kills the defender before they can strike back.

Untouchable

Cannot be targetted by effects.

Eager

The creature can attack on the turn it is summoned.
